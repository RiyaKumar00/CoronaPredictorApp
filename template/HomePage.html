Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), a highly infectious disease, was first detected in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. The disease has spread to 212 countries and territories around the world and infected (confirmed) more than forty-six million people. In India, the disease was first detected on 30 January 2020 in Kerala in a student who returned from Wuhan.While coronaviruses are common, coronavirus (COVID-19) is a new strain of coronavirus that had previously not been identified in humans. The key features of COVID-19 are respiratory symptoms with a fever and cough.

The virus is spread through respiratory droplets produced by an infected person through coughing and sneezing or by touching surfaces with the virus on them. Currently there are more than eleven million active cases in the world. With such a widespread, it becomes necessary to analyse the growth pattern of this disease and predict its future growth to make required arrangements in our resources to handle the worst situation. This is exactly what our application does! Using the latest algorithms in Machine Learning and a huge database to support our models, We can predict the number of cases in each state for the next seven days.

Along with predicting the number of cases in your state, we also provide a simple online test that will analyse your symptoms and help you in deciding whether to seek medical help or not.
